## Text Embeddings

Generate vector representations of text for semantic search, clustering, and similarity matching.

| Model | Model ID | Input | Output |
|-------|----------|-------|--------|
| [BGE-M3](https://huggingface.co/KimChen/bge-m3-GGUF) | `text-embedding-bge-m3` | $0.15 per 1M tokens | $0.60 per 1M tokens |

---

## Use Cases

- **Semantic Search** — Find relevant documents based on meaning, not just keywords
- **Clustering** — Group similar texts together automatically
- **RAG** — Enhance LLM responses with relevant context
- **Deduplication** — Identify and remove duplicate content
